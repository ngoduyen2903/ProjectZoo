Note when buying tickets online.

- If you purchase your ticket online, after payment, the QR Code will be sent to your EMAIL as soon as the transaction has been successfully confirmed.

- When you enter the automatic gate, you just need to scan the QR code CODE you receive. You can donate tickets to friends / relatives by sending QR Code to recipients easily.

- Each QR Code after scanning will lose its use value - The ticket is valid for 30 days (from the date of purchase)

- Buy tickets Online to be active in time, save time waiting for tickets during busy times

- Children under 3 years old are free
